Products in the Oil and Gas Sector

1. Exploration and Production

The exploration and production sector involves activities related to finding and extracting oil and gas reserves from the earth. It includes:

  • Seismic surveys
  • Drilling and well construction
  • Production platforms
  • Subsea systems

2. Refining and Processing

Refining and processing involves converting crude oil and natural gas into refined products. This sector includes:

  • Refineries
  • Petrochemical plants
  • Gas processing facilities

3. Transportation and Storage

Transportation and storage involves moving and storing oil and gas products. It includes:

  • Pipelines
  • Tankers and vessels
  • Storage terminals
